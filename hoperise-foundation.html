About Us

Driven by Purpose, Guided by Communities

Founded in 2010, HopeRise Foundation is a grassroots-led NGO committed to ending cycles of poverty through long-term, sustainable community development. We work alongside local leaders, not above them.

From rural schools in sub-Saharan Africa to flood-prone villages in South Asia, we tailor every program to local needs — creating lasting change, not temporary relief.

🎯

Community-Led

Solutions designed by and for the communities we serve.

📊

Data-Driven

Every decision is backed by evidence and field research.

♻️

Sustainable

Programs built to last long after our teams leave.

🔍

Transparent

Full financial accountability to donors and partners.

Programs That Transform Lives

Six core program areas aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, delivering measurable results in communities that need it most.

Building schools, training teachers, and providing scholarships for 24,000+ children in underserved regions annually.
